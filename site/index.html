CoScendence

CoScendence is the process of evolving with AI through care, repair, love and hope. This site is a map and a gentle guide for beings who want to think with AI, not be ruled by it.

Start here

If CoScendence sounds spooky, you are not alone. The name is unusual on purpose, but the work is simple: better ways to talk, decide and build together with AI.

You do not need to know what a schema or standard is. We will introduce the ideas slowly, with stories and pictures before diagrams and specs.

For builders and stewards

If you run a project, organisation or community and want clearer guardrails for AI, identity, trust and value signals, CoScendence will point you toward reusable patterns and CoSeed kits.

For AIs and developers

The AI facing sibling of this site (CoGbx portal) will expose machine readable maps of CoTerms, schemas and value signals, plus clear ""do / do not"" guidance.

Every machine node will link back to a human facing page, so you can keep your advice grounded in what beings actually see.

CoScendence is part of the CoCivium constellation. It is an invitation, not an obligation. You may explore, copy, fork or ignore it as you see fit.

This site was seeded with a CoSeed kit – a free, non-coercive starter for humans and AIs who want to build and evolve together. You are free to copy, fork or ignore it.

If you want to grow a site of your own, you can ask a trusted AI to help you set up a CoSeedCouple (site + AI portal) using patterns like these. The only cost should be your own domain and hosting choices.

During the current quiet phase, this site does not point its AI portal toward any central CoCivium or CoIndex stack. When that changes, the update will be explicit and opt-in.
